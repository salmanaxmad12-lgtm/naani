Tariiqda Salman Coffee Shop

Shaqadan waxa bilaawnay ani io group aan asxaab nhy fikrada shaqadaan waxa keenty dumashidiina waxaana bilaawny shaqadaan sandka markuu aha 2006.

Himiladayada & Aragtidayada

Aragtida (Vision):
Inaan noqono coffee shop-ka ugu caansan ee Muqdisho, meesha dadka ay ku nastaan oo ay ku helaan kafeega ugu macaan.
Himilada (Mission):
Inaan macaamiisheena siino kafeega tayada sare leh, cabitaanno kala duwan, iyo adeeg saaxiibtinimo leh oo joogto ah.
"Kafeega wanaagsan iyo adeegga saaxiibtinimo leh ayaa ka dhigaya Cozy Salman Coffee Shop meel ay macaamiishu mar walba ku laabtaan." - Maamulaha Cozy Salman Coffee Shop.
